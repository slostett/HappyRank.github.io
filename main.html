Our Story

HappyRank was intially founded to assist medical schools with their third year clinical rotation assignment process. Since then, we've expanded to run matches for multiple graduate and professional schools nationwide.


Since students are often required to move for their training, matching according to their preferences is crucial to ensure their happiness - and minimize administrator headache. Often, lottery based systems are used to place students. These systems are inefficient and tend to place students semi-randomly.


Our solution is based on a proprietary algorithm and balances student happiness across a class. Rather than locking in student placements one by one, students are placed flexibly so that swaps increasing happiness globally can be made. Don't take our word for it - read on to see figures from real matches and testimonials from satisfied students.
